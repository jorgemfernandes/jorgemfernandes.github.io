Jorge M. Fernandes

Political Scientist

Welcome to my website! I am a Ramón y Cajal Research Fellow at the Institute of Public Goods and Policies at CSIC, Madrid.

These days, I spend most of my time researching on comparative political institutions, with a particular emphasis on legislatures, political parties, legislative debates, and coalitions.

I hold a PhD in Political and Social Sciences from the European University Institute.
